We use collected information to provide mileage tracking, create summaries and exports, improve product reliability, respond to support requests, and meet legal obligations.

Location data is used to calculate mileage and support task-time trip records. Because this data can be sensitive, we aim to limit collection to what is reasonably required for the service.

About us

We build tools that respect how gig workers actually operate.

Gigstrak exists to remove guesswork from mileage tracking. Our focus is simple: make it easier for independent workers to protect deductions, understand their day, and maintain records they can trust.

Our mission

Too many workers lose money because mileage tracking is inconsistent, distracting, or impossible to maintain during a real shift. We are building a cleaner standard: professional tools designed around movement, speed, and accountability.

What we value

Accuracy, clarity, and restraint. We believe productivity software should be calm, credible, and useful under pressure. Every product decision should support trust at the moment of work and later during reconciliation.

Who it serves

Delivery drivers, rideshare workers, errand runners, field service teams, and any independent professional whose vehicle is part of the job.
